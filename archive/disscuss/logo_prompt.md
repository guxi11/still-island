# Nano Banana 绘图指导 - Porthole iOS App Logo

## 设计理念
Porthole是一个数字健康应用，通过画中画技术在你的屏幕上开一扇小窗，温柔提醒你屏幕之外还有更重要的事。Logo需要体现：舷窗内外对比、光线透出、数字与现实的平衡。

## Prompt内容
```
Generate a professional iOS app logo for a digital wellness app called "Porthole" with the following specifications:

1. **Composition & Layout**
   - A rounded rectangle window (representing a porthole) with NO border
   - Place the window so that its lower-right corner is at the center of the logo
   - The window should not be too close to the visual center or edges
   - Use a pure flat design style (no realistic textures, 3D effects, or shadows)
   - Inside the window: a solid, rich golden yellow color filling the entire window
   - From the window: a much lighter, realistic beam of light streaming toward the lower-right corner
   - The light should feel like real life, natural and gentle, not artificial

2. **Color Palette** (professional, harmonious, following color theory)
   - **OUTSIDE THE WINDOW**: A harmonious, contemplative deep blue (#2C3E50) - represents digital immersion
   - **WINDOW INTERIOR**: Solid, rich golden yellow (#FFB300) - represents the warmth of real life
   - **LIGHT BEAM**: Much lighter, natural light color (#FFE5B4 → #FFF5E1) - represents reality shining through
   - **NO BORDER**: The window has no border - pure shape against background
   - **COLOR HARMONY**: The golden yellow (#FFB300) and deep blue (#2C3E50) are complementary colors on the color wheel, creating a harmonious, balanced contrast

3. **Visual Contrast & Balance** (using color theory principles)
   - Strong but harmonious contrast between the deep blue exterior and golden yellow interior
   - The light beam is significantly lighter than the window color, creating a natural transition
   - No shadows, glows, or halo effects (pure flat design)
   - The light should feel like a natural, gentle illumination from the window
   - The composition is balanced: window's lower-right at center creates visual stability

4. **Technical Requirements**
   - Square canvas: 1024×1024 pixels
   - **Transparent background**: PNG format with alpha channel
   - Vector-friendly design (scalable to 512×512, 256×256, etc.)
   - Clean, sharp edges on window shape
   - Light beam has soft, natural gradient
   - No text, just visual symbol

5. **Style & Emotion**
   - Pure flat design (no skeuomorphism, borders, or decorative elements)
   - Professional and harmonious aesthetic
   - Natural, gentle and uplifting (real life warmth)
   - Timeless and balanced
   - Minimalist: simplicity meets meaningful contrast

6. **Conceptual Interpretation**
   - The borderless rounded rectangle represents a pure digital portal/window
   - The deep blue exterior represents digital immersion and contemplation
   - The solid golden yellow interior represents the warmth and richness of real life
   - The lighter light beam represents reality gently shining through into digital space
   - The centered lower-right positioning creates balanced, grounded composition
   - Transparency emphasizes the "window to reality" metaphor

7. **Avoid These**
   - No borders, frames, or outlines around the window
   - No glow effects, halos, or shadows
   - No unrealistic or artificial light qualities
   - No harsh color clashes - colors must be harmonious
   - No overly complex patterns or gradients
   - No 3D effects, textures, or decorative elements
   - Keep it simple, balanced, and meaningful
```

## 中文解释要点（供参考）

### 构图布局
1. **窗口位置**：窗口的右下角位于logo中心，不靠近视觉中心也不靠近边缘，保持平衡感
2. **窗口设计**：圆角矩形（圆角半径约15%），无边框设计，简洁纯净
3. **窗口颜色**：舷窗内完全由饱满的金黄色填满（#FFB300）
4. **光线颜色**：射出的光颜色要浅很多（#FFE5B4 → #FFF5E1），像真实生活中的自然光
5. **光线质感**：自然渐变，真实生活感，无人工痕迹

### 配色原理
1. **窗外色**：和谐沉思深蓝（#2C3E50），代表数字沉浸，与金黄色形成补色关系
2. **窗内色**：饱满金黄（#FFB300），代表现实生活的温暖和丰富性
3. **光线色**：浅自然光（#FFE5B4 → #FFF5E1），代表现实生活的自然透入
4. **色彩学原理**：金黄色（#FFB300）和深蓝（#2C3E50）在色轮上是补色，遵循色彩学原理，对比和谐不冲突
5. **光线过渡**：从金黄到浅黄的自然渐变，符合真实光线的物理特性
6. **禁止效果**：无光晕、无阴影、无边框，保持纯粹

### 技术细节
1. **透明背景**：PNG格式，支持App Store所有尺寸要求
2. **尺寸要求**：1024×1024基础，可缩放至512、256、128等
3. **窗口位置**：通过让窗口右下角在logo中心实现平衡定位
4. **边缘处理**：窗口边缘清晰无边框，光线边缘自然渐变
5. **颜色规范**：使用准确的HEX色值，确保颜色和谐
6. **禁止效果**：严格禁止任何光晕、阴影、3D效果

### 风格情绪
1. **专业感**：现代扁平设计，遵循色彩学原理
2. **自然感**：光线如真实生活般自然，无人工痕迹
3. **平衡感**：通过中心定位实现视觉平衡
4. **纯净感**：无边框、无装饰的简洁设计
5. **温暖感**：金黄色带来的现实生活温暖感
6. **和谐感**：颜色搭配遵循色彩学原理，对比而不冲突

## 使用说明
1. 将上述英文prompt直接复制到Nano Banana工具中
2. 选择「扁平设计」或「图标设计」模式
3. 确保输出为透明PNG格式
4. 可能需要微调：光线自然度、颜色和谐度、窗口位置精确度

## 备选方案
如果希望更简洁的表现：
- 窗口可简化为纯粹的圆角矩形色块
- 光线可简化为单色浅黄渐变
- 保持无边框、无光晕的核心原则
- 确保颜色和谐：金黄与深蓝的补色关系
